Details

Enjoy Himchari National Park in all its natural glory with the magnificent view of hills, waterfall and the sea- a perfect hub for nature lovers. Inani Beach is one of the most wonderful coral beaches with blue sea spread across the horizon, that creates a breathtaking view for experiencing both sunrise and sunset.

Itinerary

Arrival at Himchari National Park 09:00 AM

Arrival at Inani Beach 10:00 AM

One of the finest beaches spread across an area of 18km. Find yourself amidst fascinating coral stones. In the summer and rainy season these coral stones take on greenly shade. The beach is particularly very popular for these rocks and boulders. Enjoy a particularly stunning view of the sunrise and sunset with hills on one side and the sea on the other.

Arrival at Patuartek Beach 11:00 AM

One can find inner peace here. It’s different from any-other beaches in Cox’s Bazar. There is Green Hills on one side and there is a wide-open blue sea on the other. Innumerable numbers of Coral, Stones, Rocks, and Black Sand are the main attractions here. Duration: 30 minutes

End of Tour 12:00 AM

Drop off at hotel or preferred location in Laboni, Sugandha, Kolatoli point.

Policy

Cancellation

  • To cancel any tour, an email has to be sent to tours@gozayaan.com mentioning the tour booking ID and details about the cancellation.
  • The time of sending the email will be considered as the time of cancellation.
  • The email will be considered as the final application for cancellation. A phone call to the Go Zayaan hotline number or any other team member of Go Zayaan will not be considered as a request for cancellation.

Refund

  • The full amount of the tour fee will be refunded if the booking is canceled twenty-four (24) hours prior to the start of the experience/tour.
  • No refund will be provided if the booking is canceled less than twenty-four (24) hours prior to the start of the experience/tour.

  • All refunds will be processed within seven (7) working days.
